HOMEGROUND!





HOMEGROUND is South Africa's biggest and hottest outdoor kasi gathering within a township, which takes place over one day on the first weekend of every third month.

From the first day of the weekend of each month festival-goers can expect a multi-faceted and multi-sensory action-packed day and night featuring the best local and international entertainment across the music industry, top local acts and some of the world's most inspiring artists performing on multiple stages; creating good times and making incredible memories.
